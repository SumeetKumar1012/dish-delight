Chana Masala

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Add soaked chickpeas and cook with water for 20 minutes.
  4. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, then serve with rice or bread.
