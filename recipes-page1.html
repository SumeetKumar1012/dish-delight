Cocktail Meatballs

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey

Instructions

  1. Mix beef, breadcrumbs, egg, onions, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Shape into small meatballs and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes.
  3. In a saucepan, mix BBQ sauce and honey, then simmer.
  4. Add meatballs to the sauce and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with toothpicks for easy handling.
