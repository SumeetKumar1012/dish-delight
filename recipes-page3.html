Chickpea Tikka Masala

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Saute onions in a pan until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until aromatic.
  3. Stir in chickpeas and coconut milk; simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice or naan.
