Crispy Vegetable Pakoras

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1 potato, grated
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix chickpea flour with all spices and vegetables.
  2. Slowly add water to form a thick batter.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, drop spoonfuls of batter, and fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve with chutney or yogurt.
