Bihari Kabab

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 500g beef or chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt, onions, and spices for at least 4 hours.
  2. Thread the marinated meat onto skewers.
  3. Grill or pan-fry the skewers until cooked through and golden brown.
  4. Serve with naan or salad.
