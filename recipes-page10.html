Indian Lassi

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend yogurt, water, sugar, and cardamom powder until smooth.
  2. Serve chilled with ice cubes if desired.
