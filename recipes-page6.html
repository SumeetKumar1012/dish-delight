Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until translucent.
  2. Add tomatoes, curry powder, cumin, and cook until soft.
  3. Add sweet potatoes and chickpeas, cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  5. Serve warm with rice or naan.
