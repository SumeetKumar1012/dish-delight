Naan

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Butter for brushing

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Add yogurt and warm water to make a dough.
  2. Knead the dough and let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out the dough into small circles and cook in a hot pan, flipping once.
  4. Brush with melted butter and serve warm.
