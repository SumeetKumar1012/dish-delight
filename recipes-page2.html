Carrot Halwa (Gajar Ka Halwa)

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrots, grated
  • 1 liter milk
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g ghee
  • 50g chopped nuts
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Heat milk in a pan and add grated carrots.
  2. Cook until milk reduces, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add sugar, ghee, and cardamom powder. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  4. Garnish with nuts and serve warm.
