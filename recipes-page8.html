Indian-Style Seekh Kebab

Home Page 1 Page 2 About Us Contact Us

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix ground meat with onion, spices, and coriander.
  2. Form the mixture into sausage-like shapes on skewers.
  3. Grill or cook on a pan until fully cooked and browned.
  4. Serve with naan or salad.
